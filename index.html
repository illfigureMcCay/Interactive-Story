"Morning begins like every morning, the orange color of dawn bleeds into another new day as a kingdom guard. Emil never once questioned what he did. He protected his village outpost with no hesitation. The same morning, he soon found that everyone was gone without a trace. Only the weapons and the guards' belongings were left. As he stood in the empty outpost, Emil then faintly heard something odd past the border where the outpost lie. Booming sounds that drew closer, as if a huge mass of people were heading this way. He had to make a choice as soon as possible. There was enough time to retreat away from the village, time to run away completely, or to stay and defend the village where he stands."
